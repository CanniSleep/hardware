Lenovo thinkpad T14 Gen 2:
RAM: 16 GB
CPU: AMD Ryzen 7 Pro
GPU: AMD Radeon Graphics 
Harddisk: 256 GB SSD

Checks via voidlinux live usb:
GPU: lspci | grep -E "VGA|3D"
CPU: lscpu
RAM: free -h
Wifi card: lspci -k | grep -A 3 -i network
• Note: look for intel not realtk
Harddisk: lsblk -o NAME,ROTA,SIZE,TYPE,MODEL
Battery: cat /sys/class/power_supply/BAT0/uevent
If not BAT0 just ls power_supply dir
Wlan: ip link
• Note: if delays after lenovo 10 sec or stuck st lenovo need firmware update at home (fwupd)
At home:
Display:from BIOS get serial number then (support.lenovo.com/parts-lookup) As-built tab Display: Filter by "LCD Panels". Look for 300nits or 400nits Low Power.
+ thermal paste every year
